TODO APP

Project information

  • Category: App design
  • Client: Yochanan Israel
  • Project date: 16 September, 2022
  • Project URL: http://127.0.0.1:5502/index.html

A simple todo app built with HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Features:

  1. You can personalize your TODO APP by adding your name.
  2. You can create a task and save it securely.
  3. You can add due date for each todo.
  4. You can add due time for each todo.
  5. You can sort your todos alphabetically.
  6. You can divide your todos by category.
  7. You can edit your todos.
  8. You can delete your todos.
